Rental Policy

Welcome! Please read our rental policy carefully before renting any products or services from us.

1. Rental Duration

All rentals are for the period agreed upon at the time of booking. Extensions must be requested and approved before the original rental period ends.

2. Payment

  • Full payment or deposit is required to confirm the rental.
  • Payments can be made via our approved payment methods.

3. Item Condition

Rented items must be returned in the same condition they were provided. Any damages or loss will be charged accordingly.

4. Cancellation & Refund

Cancellations made at least 24 hours before the rental period will be eligible for a refund. Late cancellations or no-shows may not be refunded.

5. Responsibility

The renter is responsible for the safe use and timely return of the rented items.
